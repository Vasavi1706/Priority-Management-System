About Us

This project aims to develop a web-based complaint system for the college campus, specifically designed for B. Tech hostel students.The proposed system enables the users to submit their complaints specifying the urgency level and description. A rule-based priority ranking mechanism evaluates the complaint based on the urgency level and frequency of complaints recorded. The highest ranked complaints are solved immediately and satisfies the students utmost requirements.This system improves efficiency, transparency, and responsiveness in complaint management.
